Our Story

Started with one wardrobe. Grown one referral at a time.

D Interior began with fitted wardrobe and closet work for homes around Basanta Nagar, Dibrugarh. Word travelled through happy clients before it travelled through advertising, and the studio grew into full bedroom, kitchen and living-room fit-outs while keeping the same small, hands-on team involved in every project — from the first sketch to the final coat of finish.

Vision

Interiors that outlast trends.

To be the studio Upper Assam homeowners trust first — for interiors built on real craftsmanship, not passing styles that need redoing in five years.

Mission

Understand the home before we design it.

To design and build every wardrobe, bedroom and room fit-out around how the household actually uses it — on time, on budget, and finished to a standard we'd put in our own homes.

Upper Assam, By Design

Why local context changes the design brief.

Dibrugarh's humidity and monsoon season shape real decisions — moisture-resistant carcass materials for wardrobes and kitchens, finishes that don't warp through the wet months, and layouts that work with the tin-roofed and RCC home types common across Upper Assam. Warm wood tones and brass detailing also read particularly well against the tea-garden light this region is known for, which is part of why they anchor our palette.
